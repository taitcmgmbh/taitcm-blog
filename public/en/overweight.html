Natural Weight Management – Boosting Metabolism with TCM

1. How does TCM view overweight?

In TCM, being overweight is often an expression of Spleen-Qi deficiency with dampness accumulation. Liver-Qi stagnation and Kidney-Yang deficiency can also be involved—depending on your constitution.

2. Does acupuncture help with weight loss?

Yes. It reduces food cravings, regulates digestive fire, and promotes fat metabolism. It is particularly effective when combined with tailored nutrition and exercise.

3. What are the benefits of herbal therapy?

It supports weight loss from within—by strengthening digestion, promoting drainage, and reducing stress. Ideal for "invisible weight gain" despite dieting.

4. Who is TCM suitable for?

For everyone wishing to lose weight without crash diets—especially for hormonal problems, fatigue, water retention, or emotional eating.

5. How long does treatment take?

Typically, 4–6 weeks are required for initial results. TCM works gently but sustainably—leading to better quality of life and less risk of relapse.
