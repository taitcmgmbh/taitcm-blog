Natural Relief for Achilles Tendon Symptoms – The TCM Solution

1. How does TCM view issues with the Achilles tendon?

In TCM, these are considered "Tendon Bi" or muscular blockages—caused by overuse, sports injuries, cold, or chronic weakness. Active individuals or those who stand or run for long periods are particularly affected.

2. Does acupuncture help with Achilles tendon pain?

Yes. Acupuncture reduces inflammation, promotes microcirculation, and supports tissue healing. It is effective for both acute and chronic conditions.

3. Is Tuina massage recommended?

Once acute inflammation has subsided, Tuina is helpful for loosening tense calf muscles and relieving the tendon. It is ideal when used in combination with acupuncture.

4. How do Chinese herbs support healing?

Herbal therapies act as anti-inflammatories, promote blood circulation, and strengthen tendons and ligaments. They can be administered as teas, capsules, or plasters.

5. What should be considered in daily life?

Avoid jumping and sprints during the recovery phase. Heat applications, stretching exercises, and appropriate footwear promote regeneration and help prevent relapses.
