Natural Relief for Achilles Tendon Pain – TCM Treatment in Zurich

Achilles tendon pain is often caused by overuse, sports injuries, or muscle imbalance. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) treats these conditions using acupuncture, Tuina massage, and herbal therapy to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and support natural healing.

1. How does TCM understand Achilles tendon problems?

In TCM, Achilles tendon pain is often classified as "Tendon Bi syndrome", caused by stagnation of Qi and blood, cold exposure, or overuse. Athletes and people who stand or walk for long periods are especially affected.

2. Can acupuncture help with Achilles tendon pain?

Yes. Acupuncture improves circulation, reduces inflammation, and activates the body’s self-healing mechanisms. It is effective for both acute irritation and chronic tendon problems.

3. Is Tuina massage useful?

Tuina helps relax tight calf muscles and reduce strain on the tendon. It is particularly effective when combined with acupuncture, especially after acute inflammation has subsided.

4. What role do Chinese herbs play?

Chinese herbal medicine can reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and strengthen tendons and ligaments. Treatments are tailored individually and may be prescribed as granules or teas.

5. What should you consider in daily life?

During recovery, avoid jumping, sprinting, and excessive strain. Gentle stretching, warmth, and proper footwear can support healing and help prevent recurrence.
