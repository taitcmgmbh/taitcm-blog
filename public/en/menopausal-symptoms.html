Natural Relief for Menopausal Symptoms – The TCM Approach

1. How does TCM view menopause?

TCM understands menopause as a natural transition where an imbalance between Yin and Yang occurs—particularly through Kidney-Yin deficiency and Liver-Qi stagnation.

2. What are the benefits of acupuncture?

Acupuncture reduces hot flashes, stabilizes mood, improves sleep, and regulates the autonomic nervous system—entirely without side effects.

3. Are herbs a hormone-free alternative?

Yes. Chinese herbs strengthen Kidney-Yin, calm the mind, cool heat, and support adaptation—individually tailored to your constitution.

4. Is TCM a substitute for hormone therapy?

For functional complaints, yes. Many women prefer to avoid hormonal treatment—TCM offers a gentle and effective alternative with a holistic approach.

5. How long does the treatment take?

Usually 2–3 months or longer. TCM focuses on stability and prevention—alongside acupuncture and herbs, this includes nutritional and lifestyle counseling.
