Sleep Disorder Treatment in Zurich – Acupuncture & TCM

Sleep disorders are common and can affect both physical and mental well-being. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) offers a natural and holistic approach using acupuncture and herbal therapy to improve sleep quality and restore inner balance.

1. How does TCM view sleep disorders?

In TCM, sleep problems are often linked to internal imbalance, emotional stress, or stagnation of Qi. A disturbed "Shen" (mind/spirit) means the body cannot settle into restful sleep.

2. Does acupuncture help with sleep?

Acupuncture calms the nervous system, balances Heart and Kidney energy, and relaxes the mind. It is effective for difficulty falling asleep, waking up at night, and restless sleep.

3. Are there natural herbal alternatives?

Chinese herbs are gentle, non-addictive, and work long-term by reducing stress, nourishing the blood, and calming the mind.

4. Does it help with stress and overthinking?

Yes. TCM reduces internal tension, supports emotional balance and helps the mind to relax, making it easier to fall asleep naturally.

5. How quickly does treatment work?

Many patients notice improvement after a few sessions. For lasting results, a short treatment course over several weeks is often recommended.
