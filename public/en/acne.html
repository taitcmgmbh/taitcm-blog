2. Can acupuncture help?

Acupuncture regulates hormones, reduces stress, and improves circulation.

3. Herbal treatment?

Chinese herbs reduce inflammation and support detoxification.

4. Role of stress?

Stress worsens acne significantly. TCM helps both physically and emotionally.

5. Diet?

Reduce sugar and dairy. Focus on cooked vegetables and hydration.
