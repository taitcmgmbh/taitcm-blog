Natural Relief for Pregnancy Complaints – Gentle Help through TCM

1. What complaints occur during pregnancy?

Typical issues include: nausea, vomiting, and fatigue in the 1st trimester; back pain and digestive problems in the 2nd trimester; sleep disorders, water retention, and inner restlessness in the 3rd trimester.

2. Is acupuncture safe during pregnancy?

Yes. With expert implementation, it is very safe and helpful—e.g., for nausea, fatigue, back pain, sleep problems, or emotional imbalance.

3. Is it allowed to take herbs?

With medical guidance, yes. Particularly mild, calming, and nourishing herbs are used—no strong laxatives or blood-moving agents. Safety is the top priority.

4. Does TCM help with sleep problems and restlessness?

Yes. Acupuncture and herbs regulate Heart and Liver Qi, calm the mind, and promote deep, restful sleep—entirely without medication.

5. Can TCM accompany the entire pregnancy?

Absolutely. Many women use TCM for support from conception to childbed—for stabilization, preparation for birth, and to promote natural regeneration.
