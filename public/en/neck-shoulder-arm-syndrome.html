Relieving Neck-Shoulder-Arm Pain Naturally – The TCM Solution

1. What is Neck-Shoulder-Arm Syndrome?

This condition refers to complaints arising from tension or irritation in the cervical spine area, radiating into the shoulder and arm. Symptoms range from stiffness and pain to tingling and loss of strength.

2. How does acupuncture help with Cervical Syndrome?

Acupuncture relaxes deep-seated muscles, reduces inflammation, improves blood circulation, and can alleviate nerve irritation. It is particularly effective for computer work, stress, and chronic poor posture.

3. Is Tuina massage useful?

Yes. Tuina promotes mobility of the cervical spine, dissolves muscle hardening, and can reduce pressure on irritated nerves. It is very effective when combined with acupuncture.

4. When are herbs helpful?

For chronic or recurring complaints caused by cold, blood stagnation, or weakness, Chinese herbs support sustainable regulation and pain relief.

5. How often should treatment be performed?

1–2 sessions per week for 2–3 weeks are often sufficient. Additionally, self-care is important: warmth, posture awareness, and regular stretching exercises.
