Back Pain Treatment in Zurich – Acupuncture & TCM

Back pain is one of the most common complaints in daily life. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) offers a holistic approach using acupuncture, Tuina massage and herbal therapy to relieve pain, relax tense muscles and support long-term recovery.

1. How does TCM understand back pain?

In TCM, back pain is often related to stagnation of Qi and blood, external influences such as cold or dampness, poor posture, overuse or emotional tension. The back is closely connected with the Bladder meridian, the Du Mai and Kidney energy.

2. How can acupuncture help?

Acupuncture helps relax tense muscles, activate circulation and reduce pain. It is especially useful for stress-related tension, computer work, muscular overload and recurring lower back pain.

3. Are Tuina massage and cupping useful?

Yes. Tuina massage helps release tight muscles and fascia restrictions, while cupping promotes local blood circulation. Many patients feel noticeable relief shortly after treatment.

4. When are Chinese herbs helpful?

For chronic back pain, cold sensitivity or recurring symptoms, Chinese herbs may support circulation, reduce inflammation and strengthen the body’s recovery process.

5. What treatment plan is recommended?

For acute pain, 3–5 sessions within a short period are often recommended. For chronic back pain, regular treatments combined with movement, posture correction and daily self-care are usually more effective.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly does acupuncture work?
Many patients notice improvement after a few sessions, depending on the cause and duration of the pain.

Is the treatment painful?
Acupuncture is generally well tolerated and often experienced as relaxing.
